All the aggregated information that we provide to our third-party partners (like companies, organizations or individuals) are based on an aggregated statistics or demographics, and insights that help third-party partners (like companies, organizations or individuals) to understand how users like you are engaging with their ads, listings, pages, videos or any other content.

Aggregated information that we disclosed with any third-party partner could be used to tailor specific advertisement, special promotions, or to be use for any commercial purpose that we deem reasonable in accordance with our privacy policy and terms of use. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
We understand the importance of protecting children’s privacy in the interactive world. Our services are restricted to users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit users under the age of 18 on our platform. If you suspect that a user is under the age of 18, please use the reporting mechanism available through the service.

We do not use or knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of thirteen (13). If you are a child under 13 years of age, you are not permitted to use Emvite and should not send any information about yourself to us through the Service.

In the event that we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from any child, we will dispose of that information in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. 6501-05, or any other applicable laws or regulations.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child under the age of 13 has provided us with information without your consent, please contact us at childprivacy@emvite.com and we will take reasonable steps to ensure that such information is deleted from our files.
