About this app

Welcome to emVite, a location-based social search engine and networking community to help you connect to new people around you who share your interests by simply choosing and interacting through specific emojis that describe your real time interests, hobbies or activities.

• Have coffee, brunch, enjoy a day at the beach or simply chat – with more than 25+ emojis, emVite will get you close to those with your same interest.

• Discover new people with same interest - just go public to meet new friends and emVite away.

• emVite personal contacts and friends - through this platform you can get together with known ones from your mobile list of contacts just by sending an emoji with a short message.

• User friendly and fun to use - just pick an emoji which represents your interest and look for other people, emVite and chat.

• Live or schedule emVitation – look for people nearby or simply your activity for later.

• Places – recommendations of places near you.

• Location – search for people around you for up to 100 miles.

Why emvite

emVite allows you to instantly connect and chat with those who share your interests and would like to do the same things as you. Be it a search for a new exercise partner, a fellow foodie to check out the local food scene, finding a wingman to explore the outdoors or hit up the nearby bike trails, or just someone to have coffee with and talk about life, emVite is here to help you stir up your social life. So the next time your favorite artist is in town just fire up emVite and find someone who would be thrilled to go there with you. You never know, perhaps that one evening will turn into a lifelong friendship.

How it works

Simply decide what your interest is by selecting one or multiple emojis to immediately start connecting and emViting likeminded people. emVite allows the end-user to accept and or decline emVitations, and to live-chat on-demand to determine the ideal date, time, and meeting location.

Select one or more emoji to discover and emVite new people near you in real-time that simple; meet & greet or instantly emVite people from your mobile phone contacts either individually or as a group emVite for any type of get together.

User Account

Register as an individual user and create a personalized profile that enables other users to quickly find and emVite you to join them to participate in a range of diverse activities through the selection of emojis in a predetermined location radius.

Each emoji represents a particular and current desire, want, craving, mood, and/or hobby of interest. With more than 25+ available emojis to choose from, there is no reason to do anything unaccompanied.


Business Account

Register as a business to enable users to quickly find you and learn about your products, services, promotions and special events within a predetermined location radius based on the emojis of choice for the business. emVite will also make nearby business suggestions to its users based on the user’s emoji of choice and location at the specific time. Registered businesses will further benefit from direct customer feedback through the emVite platform; this will streamline performance and maximize exposure in order to capture new business.
